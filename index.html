Our Marketing Process

How we get your home in front of the right buyers.

Putting a sign in the yard is easy. Getting your home positioned to earn what it's actually worth takes a different conversation — and a deliberate marketing strategy from day one.

01

Professional Photography

HDR photography, drone shots where applicable. Buyers decide whether to tour based on listing images alone — this is the first impression.

02

3D Tour & Video

A full Matterport 3D walkthrough and listing video published online. Out-of-state and busy buyers tour without leaving home.

03

Property Website

A dedicated URL for your home — professional, shareable, and designed to convert interest into showings.

04

Dual MLS Listing

Listed on both RMLS and WVMLS simultaneously. That's the entire Portland metro buyer pool — not just half of it.

05

Targeted Social

Paid social ads targeting buyers by location, income, and search behavior. Your listing reaches people actively looking — not just browsing.

06

Open Houses

Strategically scheduled opens during peak buyer activity. We handle the setup, signage, and follow-up with every visitor.
